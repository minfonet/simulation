# Vision

## Problem
Existing training systems are fragmented, expensive, unrealistic, and difficult to customize across industries.

Organizations need:
- safer training environments
- measurable evaluation systems
- realistic operational simulations
- repeatable training scenarios
- telemetry-based performance analysis
- scalable instructor management

## Solution
Build a unified AI-native simulation ecosystem capable of recreating realistic operational environments for:
- transportation
- logistics
- industrial operations
- aviation
- maritime navigation
- emergency response training

## Long-Term Vision
Become the leading institutional and enterprise simulation platform for operational training.
